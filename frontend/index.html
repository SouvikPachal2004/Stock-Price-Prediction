How Our LSTM Model Works

Our stock price prediction system leverages Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, a type of recurrent neural network (RNN) specifically designed to recognize patterns in sequences of data.

The model analyzes historical stock prices, trading volumes, and technical indicators to identify complex patterns that might be invisible to traditional analysis methods.
